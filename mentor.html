FAQ

Common Questions

How do I get matched with a mentor?

Submit your problem using the form above. Our team reviews it and matches you with the most suitable mentor based on your category and description — usually within 24–48 hours.

Is the mentorship free?

Yes! All mentorship sessions at GEC Siwan Startup Cell are completely free for enrolled students. Our mentors volunteer their time to support student innovation.

How long is a typical mentorship session?

Sessions typically last 30–60 minutes. You can schedule follow-ups based on mutual availability. Some mentors also offer async help via email or chat.

Can I switch my mentor?

Absolutely. If you feel another mentor would be a better fit, just contact us and we'll reassign you. Your growth is the priority.

What happens after I submit the form?

Our team reviews your submission, identifies the best mentor match, and emails you with their contact details and a suggested session time within 2 working days.
