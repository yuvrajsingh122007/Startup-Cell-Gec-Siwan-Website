The Process

Your Funding Roadmap

A structured path from idea to funding

01

Idea Validation

We help you validate your startup idea through market research, problem-solution fit analysis, and feedback sessions.

02

Mentorship Support

Get paired with experienced mentors from industry who guide you on business strategy, product development, and scaling.

03

Pitch Preparation

Craft a compelling pitch deck and practice your presentation skills with mock pitch sessions and expert feedback.
