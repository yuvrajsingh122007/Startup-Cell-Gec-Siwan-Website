Who We Are

Empowering Student Entrepreneurs

The Startup Cell of Government Engineering College, Siwan is a student-driven initiative dedicated to fostering innovation and entrepreneurship among budding engineers. We bridge the gap between classroom learning and real-world problem-solving.

Our cell provides a vibrant ecosystem where students can ideate, prototype, and launch ventures with the guidance of experienced mentors. We believe every student has the potential to be a changemaker — we're here to unlock it.
